TruDefender™ Section Summary (from Truman Technologies site)
-------------------------------------------------------------

**Tagline & Overview**
"TruDefender™ — Defend. Detect. Dominate."
"An AI-Driven Managed Detection & Response Platform"

TruDefender™ is a 24×7 managed detection-and-response (MDR) platform that combines AI analytics, zero-trust access, and human SOC (Security Operations Center) expertise to detect and neutralize threats proactively. It provides enterprise-grade protection with startup agility and compliance awareness.

**Purpose**
Traditional security tools are mostly reactive. TruDefender delivers proactive, managed security that offers visibility, automation, and expert action.

**Performance Metrics**
- Mean Time to Detect: <15 minutes
- Mean Time to Respond: <60 minutes
- SOC Operations: 24×7 coverage

**Workflow**
1. Connect — Integrate with endpoints, cloud, email, identity, and network sources.
2. Detect — AI and analysts turn alerts into actionable insights.
3. Respond — Automated playbooks isolate, block, and remediate threats.
4. Recover & Learn — Guided remediation and continuous improvement.

**Compliance & Standards**
Covers frameworks across major regions:

- **United States:** NIST CSF & 800-171, SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, CMMC Level 2, FedRAMP Moderate.
- **India:** DPDP Act 2023, CERT-IN Guidelines 2022, ISO 27001:2022, CIS Controls v8.
- **Middle East:** UAE NESA, Dubai DESC, Saudi NCA, Qatar QNIAP, ISO 27701.

**Key Benefits**
- 90% fewer false alerts.
- 45% lower operational cost vs in-house SOC.
- Global 24×7 coverage (India | UAE | USA).

**Target Customers**
Cloud-first enterprises, SMEs, regulated industries (Healthcare, BFSI, Government).

**Core Capabilities**
- Hybrid Intelligence (AI + Human Analysts)
- Unified Dashboard
- Plug-and-Protect Deployment
- Continuous Learning & Compliance Tracking
